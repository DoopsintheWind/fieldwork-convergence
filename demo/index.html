What this was

A compressed portfolio demo of a three-part qualitative methods sequence designed by Dinara Pisareva for PLS511 at Nazarbayev University. The full pedagogy runs over two interactive classroom sessions on photovoice and collaborative coding, then a three-hour final exam where fifteen graduate students face scenarios built from their own thesis research. The demo here keeps the shape and the core moves, in six minutes.

Demo source code is public at github.com/DoopsintheWind/fieldwork-convergence. The teacher writes at doopsinthewind.github.io.
